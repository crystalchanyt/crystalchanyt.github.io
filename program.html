Graphic Design
Unleash Your Creative Potential: Graphic Design at Yale School of Art
Courses
Fundamentals of Graphic Design
Explore the core principles of graphic design, including color theory, typography, and layout. This course lays the groundwork for understanding visual communication and the strategic use of design elements to create compelling messages.
UX/UI Design
Understand the principles of effective web design and user experience (UX). This course covers topics like responsive design, user-centered design, and accessibility, ensuring students can create websites that are both aesthetically pleasing and highly functional.
Digital Design Tools and Techniques
Master the latest software and digital tools essential for modern graphic design. From Adobe Creative Suite to interactive media, this course provides hands-on experience in creating professional-level digital artwork and designs.
Brand Identity and Logo Design
Delve into the world of branding and learn how to develop unique, memorable brand identities. This course covers the process of logo creation, from conceptualization to execution, emphasizing the role of design in marketing and brand perception.
Design Portfolio Development
Learn how to curate and present your work professionally. This course guides you through building a compelling portfolio, showcasing your skills and creativity, and preparing you for a successful career in the graphic design industry.

Eligibility Criteria
We welcome applicants who have completed their secondary education or equivalent, demonstrating a keen interest in digital media, and meeting our academic and language proficiency requirements.
Application Process
To apply, simply submit your online application form along with required documents, including academic transcripts, a personal statement, and proof of language proficiency, followed by an interview for selected candidates.
